# Customer Churn Prediction Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a multi-page Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer churn patterns and identify factors influencing customer retention.

The dashboard analyzes customer demographics, account behavior, financial patterns, and engagement metrics to understand why customers leave and which segments are at higher churn risk.

The analysis is based on a dataset of **10,000 customers** including attributes such as age, geography, tenure, credit score, balance, product usage, salary, and customer satisfaction.

---

The Power BI dashboard contains **10 analytical pages**, each focusing on a specific business perspective.

---

## Key Business Insights
• Customers with **lower tenure show higher churn probability**  
• **Low loyalty segments** have significantly higher churn rates  
• **High-income customers contribute the majority of revenue at risk**
